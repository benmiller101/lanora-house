# How to Find Us — Video Files

Drop your two MP4 walkthrough videos in this folder using these exact filenames:

- `car-park-entrance.mp4` — Walking from the public (paid) car park to the front entrance. For all general visitors and enquiries.
- `collections-entrance.mp4` — Walking to the rear collections point. For auction winners collecting items and deliveries only.

The site will automatically pick them up once they are placed here.
