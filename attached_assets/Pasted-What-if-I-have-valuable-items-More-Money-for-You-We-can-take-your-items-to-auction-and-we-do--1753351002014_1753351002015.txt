What if I have valuable items?
£ More Money for You
We can take your items to auction — and we do, when it makes sense. But from experience, we often know there's a better way. Our goal isn't just to pass your things straight to auction and hope for the best. Instead, we handle everything in-house, with guidance from Brenna, our trusted valuer and estimator. She'll talk you through what your items are worth, your options for selling (fast cash or a higher return with patience), and realistic timeframes. We don't do this just because we're nice — we do it because it makes more money for you. And when you do better, we do better. It's that simple.

Do you take a cut of the auction sales?
0% Commission
No — we take 0% from your auction proceeds. Once the auction house takes their commission, everything else goes to you. We'll only suggest auction if we think it's the best move. Otherwise, we can sell items on your behalf at a lower commission than a traditional auctioneer, giving you a better return — especially if you're willing to wait.

I want my clearance to be as environmentally friendly as possible. Can you do that?
🌍 Planet Friendly
Definitely. Most companies send waste straight to places like Bostraze or Nick Ferris and call it done. We're different. We manually sort and separate everything ourselves — because it reduces landfill, supports local reuse and resale, and yes… it makes money. It's not about pretending to be 'green' — it's real, and it works. Sustainability and profit aren't opposites — they go hand in hand.

Can I be present during the clearance?
Your Choice
Yes, absolutely. You're welcome to be there in person, or we can handle it for you while you're away. We keep you in the loop either way — with updates, photos, and full transparency.

Do you take everything?
Almost Everything
Pretty much — furniture, rubbish, appliances, recycling, sheds, attics, garages… We handle most things. If there's something unusual (like hazardous waste), just ask and we'll let you know your options.

How long does a clearance take?
1-2 Days Usually
Most clearances take a day or two, depending on the size and complexity. We'll give you a clear timeline after our initial visit or from photos you send over.

What happens to things that can't be sold?
Responsible Disposal
Anything that's unsellable gets responsibly recycled or disposed of. We go out of our way to reduce what goes to landfill, and reclaim anything with value.

Can you help with probate or bereavement clearances?
Sensitive Support
Yes, and we do it with discretion, sensitivity, and no pressure. We know how tough this process can be. We're here to make it easier, with respect for the people, memories, and stories involved.
