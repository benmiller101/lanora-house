🟦 Pallet Shipping Option

For larger or heavier items, or when multiple lots are purchased, it may be more cost-effective to send your items on a pallet rather than individually boxed parcels.

Pallet shipping is ideal for:
• stone and ceramics
• tools, machinery, metal goods
• heavy items
• bulky or oversized lots
• multiple lots being combined
• items unsafe or uneconomical to ship via parcel courier

If pallet delivery will save you money, we will notify you.
If you can receive a pallet at your address, please let us know and we can arrange this for you.
