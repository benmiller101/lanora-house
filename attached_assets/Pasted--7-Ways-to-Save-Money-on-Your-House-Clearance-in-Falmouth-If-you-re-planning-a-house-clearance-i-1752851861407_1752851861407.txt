🗒️ 7 Ways to Save Money on Your House Clearance in Falmouth

If you’re planning a house clearance in Falmouth — whether you’re moving, downsizing, or clearing out after a loved one — the costs can add up fast. But at Lanora House Clearance, we believe in keeping things affordable, honest, and eco-friendly. Here are 7 real ways we help our Falmouth customers save money (and the planet!) during a clearance.

⸻

✅ 1. We Rehome Fridges, TVs & Paint — So You Don’t Pay Tip Fees

Many clearance companies just load everything up and charge you commercial tip fees for items like fridges, TVs, and leftover paint — which can cost you £60–£130 per item! Our dedicated team does everything possible to reuse or rehome these items responsibly, so you’re not left with a big disposal bill or any nasty surprises.

⸻

✅ 2. We Repurpose Bulky Furniture — No Double Charges

It’s tempting to just throw old sofas, beds, and wardrobes in a van. But mixed waste (especially upholstered furniture) is expensive to dump. At Lanora, we have the space and connections to hold onto good quality furniture, find it a new home, or break it down properly — saving you from paying twice for mixed waste fees.

⸻

✅ 3. We Work with Local Contractors to Reuse Materials

We proudly collaborate with local businesses like Nevitt Construction to reuse non-toxic waste such as old ceramics or rubble — putting it back into the community instead of sending it to landfill. This means you avoid unnecessary mixed waste charges, and your old stuff can help build something new right here in Cornwall.

⸻

✅ 4. We Never Charge by Weight for Electronics

A lot of companies charge by the kilo for old electronics and toys — not us! Our team genuinely enjoys sorting through vintage gadgets, old toys, and retro tech to find new homes for them. So you won’t be billed extra just because you’ve got a box of old wires or games consoles in the attic.

⸻

✅ 5. We Won’t Make You Pay to Get Rid of Clothes

Clothes shouldn’t end up in landfill — and they shouldn’t cost you to get rid of either. We donate wearable clothing to local charities or community groups whenever we can. If you don’t have the time to sort it yourself, don’t worry — we’ll handle it, no extra charge.

⸻

✅ 6. We’ll Show You How to Pack to Cut Labour Costs

Did you know you can save hours of labour fees just by having certain items ready to go? If you want to keep costs down, our team will happily advise you on how to pack, what goes where, and how to prepare for a smooth, fast clearance. No hidden charges — just friendly, practical help.

⸻

✅ 7. Local Knowledge Means Local Savings

Finally — because we’re based here in Falmouth, we know the best local charities, recycling centres, and donation networks. This means more of your unwanted items are rehomed and recycled locally, not dumped at high-cost tips. It’s good for your wallet and good for our community.
