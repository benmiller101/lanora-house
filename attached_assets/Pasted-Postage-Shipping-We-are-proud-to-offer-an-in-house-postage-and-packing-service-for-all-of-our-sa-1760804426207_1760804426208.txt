How do I add postage to my invoice?
 Once the auction ends, you'll receive an invoice by email. To add shipping, simply click the request shipping option, and we'll update your invoice accordingly.

For help, please contact the office.

What if my item is lost or damaged in transit?
 For items sold for a hammer price over £250, we take full responsibility for loss or damage during transit if shipping is arranged through NLB Auctions.
This is covered under our insurance policy (UK deliveries only, up to £10,000). An excess of £250 applies per claim.

For items under £250, while we cannot accept liability, we will assist in claiming compensation from the courier if shipping was arranged by us.*

If you arrange your own courier, NLB Auctions is not responsible for any loss or damage once items have left our premises.

How long does delivery take?
 We aim to dispatch all items within 5 working days of payment, typically on the Thursday and Friday following the sale.

Please allow up to 10 working days during busy periods.
For expedited delivery, contact us for a custom quote — we’ll do our best to accommodate.

Can I track my item?
 Yes, a tracking number will be emailed to you once your parcel has been dispatched. 

Do you ship furniture?
 We do not offer in-house furniture shipping. However, we work closely with trusted local and national carriers who will be happy to quote for delivery following the auction. 

Do you ship internationally?
 Yes. Please Contact us with your lot number and full delivery address,  email enquiries@nlbauctions.com, and we’ll provide you with a tailored international quote.
