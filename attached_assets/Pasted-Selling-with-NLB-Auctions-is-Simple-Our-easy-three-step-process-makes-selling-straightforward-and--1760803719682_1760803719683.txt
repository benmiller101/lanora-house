Selling with NLB Auctions is Simple
 Our easy three-step process makes selling straightforward and stress-free.
With decades of combined expertise, our team ensures your items are accurately appraised and reach the right buyers to achieve the best possible prices.

Our modern salerooms and strong online presence give your items the visibility they deserve.


Valuation
 

The first step in selling with NLB Auctions is receiving a free valuation of your item.
You can easily submit details and images via our online valuation form — our specialists will review and provide an estimated auction value. 


Tailored Sale of Items
 

Once consigned, our team will carry out further research, professionally photograph your items, and assign both a low and high estimate.

You’ll receive a pre-sale email or letter confirming the auction date and the estimated value range of your item(s). Your Items will then be sold in the next Appropriate Sale.


Post-Sale & Payment
 

Fourteen working days after the auction, you’ll receive a post-sale statement detailing the prices your items achieved.
Your payment will be sent via bank transfer for the total amount due.
