Probate Estate Clearance Services
Compassionate, Discreet, and Professional Support from Lanora House

🧾 Document & Valuables Retrieval

Our team can assist in locating and safely handling key documents and valuable items during the clearance, including:

Wills & title deeds

Share certificates & legal papers

Postal correspondence & bank statements

Personal effects & sentimental items

Valuables, jewellery & cash

Confidential documents for shredding

Secure storage or courier delivery of documents

We treat every item with the utmost care and confidentiality.
