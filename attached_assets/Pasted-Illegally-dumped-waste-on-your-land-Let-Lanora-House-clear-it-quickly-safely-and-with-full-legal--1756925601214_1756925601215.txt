❓ FAQs – Fly Tipping in the South West

Is it really my responsibility to clear dumped waste?
Yes. Even if you didn’t dump it, you're legally responsible for removing fly-tipped waste from your private property.

Can I be fined if I don’t act?
Yes. Local authorities may fine landowners who fail to remove waste or allow ongoing environmental hazards.

Do you help with enforcement?
Yes. We collect evidence on-site (photos, packaging, serial numbers) to help identify and report the offenders.

Do you provide ongoing services?
Yes – we offer preventative solutions such as signage, deterrents, and regular monitoring for high-risk sites.
