About Lanora House

Lanora House is an up-and-coming clearance and removal company based in Hayle, Cornwall, proudly serving communities across Cornwall, Devon, and the wider South West.

Founded in 2025 by a graduate with a background in construction project management, Lanora House was born out of both ambition and purpose — a desire to create a better kind of clearance service. One that is friendly, environmentally responsible, and built around people, not just waste.

We may be a young company, but we’re driven, growing fast, and already known for our clear communication, fast turnaround, and a strong belief in doing things properly. Whether you’re clearing a shed, managing an estate, or removing commercial waste, our team is here to make the process simple, respectful, and hassle-free.

Making Space for What Matters

At Lanora House, clearance isn’t just about removal — it’s about restoring calm, order, and opportunity. Whether it's a home that needs compassion after a bereavement or a building site that needs to stay on schedule, your space is our priority.

From your first contact to the final sweep-up, you’ll deal with real people who care. You’ll be assigned a dedicated account manager, and our friendly, reliable team will ensure everything is handled with care, discretion, and professionalism.
