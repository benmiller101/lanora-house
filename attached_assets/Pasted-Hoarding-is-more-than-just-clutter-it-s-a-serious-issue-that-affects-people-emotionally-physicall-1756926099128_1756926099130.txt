✅ What Is Hoarding?

Hoarding is a recognised mental health condition, often linked with:

Depression

Anxiety

OCD (Obsessive Compulsive Disorder)

Dementia or trauma

PTSD or prolonged grief

Hoarders often collect and keep excessive amounts of:

Old clothes

Newspapers & magazines

Broken furniture or electronics

Food waste or packaging

Biohazard materials (e.g. bodily fluids, animal waste)

Rotten food or expired medications

General rubbish, recyclables, and more

These situations can pose serious fire hazards, health risks, and often lead to emotional distress for both the individual and their loved ones.
