🛠️ Our Hotel Clearance Process

1. Consultation
We begin with a walkthrough or virtual consultation to assess your hotel and clearance goals.

2. Planning
You choose a schedule that works — including off-peak or overnight options to reduce disruption.

3. Execution
Our own team (no third-party subcontractors) arrives in branded vehicles to carry out the clearance.

4. Recycling & Disposal
All materials are sorted — reusable items are donated or resold, and the rest responsibly recycled.

5. Final Clean-Up
We leave the space clear, clean, and ready for renovation, new furniture, or repurposing.

What happens to the items you remove?
Items in good condition are reused, donated, or sold. The rest are recycled — we always aim to avoid landfill.

Can you work during off-hours?
Yes — we can carry out clearance early morning, late night, or overnight to minimise disruption to guests and staff.

Do you handle hazardous materials?
Yes — our team is trained and licensed to handle and dispose of hazardous waste safely and legally.

How long does a hotel clearance take?
It depends on the size and scope of the project. We’ll give you a clear timeline during your consultation.
