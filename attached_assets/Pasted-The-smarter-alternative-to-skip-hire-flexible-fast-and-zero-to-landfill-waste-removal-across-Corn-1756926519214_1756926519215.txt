🧱 What Is Wait and Load?

Instead of hiring a traditional skip that sits outside your property, our team arrives with a spacious truck, waits while you load it, and leaves with your waste immediately.

This means:

✅ No skip permit needed

✅ No mess left behind

✅ No risk of others filling your skip

✅ Fast turnaround with same-day slots available

It's a simple, hassle-free solution for quick waste removal – especially in busy areas or restricted sites.

💬 Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to load the van myself?
Yes — this service is self-load, but we also offer labour-assisted clearances if you'd like us to do the heavy lifting.

Can I use Wait and Load for business waste?
Absolutely. It’s ideal for offices, shops, builders, and landlords needing fast and flexible waste removal.

Do I need a permit?
No. Since the truck doesn’t stay on-site, there’s no need for a council permit.

How long do I have to load it?
Typically 30–60 minutes. If you need more time or have a large amount to shift, let us know when booking.
