1. Introduction
Lanora House is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you use our services, visit our website, create an account, or interact with us on third-party platforms like Facebook, TikTok, Google, Stripe, and others.

Name, address, email, phone number.

Payment information (processed securely via Stripe, PayPal, Klarna, etc.).

Communications via email, website forms, or phone.

IP address, browser type, device information.

Information from Third-Party Platforms:
If you engage with us via Facebook, TikTok, Google, or similar platforms, we may receive:

Your name, contact details, profile information (as permitted by platform settings).

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

✅ To provide our services (valuations, clearances, purchases, sales).
✅ To process payments securely via third-party providers.
✅ To communicate with you about orders, valuations, services, or updates.
✅ For marketing and promotions (with your consent or where permitted).
✅ For analytics and business improvement, including tracking website and advertising performance.
✅ To comply with legal obligations (e.g., fraud prevention, accounting).

Legal obligations: Compliance with laws (tax, accounting).

Consent: Where required (e.g., marketing opt-ins, cookies).
